Learn how to work with Kubernetes clusters using Lens K8S IDE and how to govern AI agents on enterprise systems with Lens Agents. Manage your personal account with Lens ID, and administer organization accounts with Lens Business ID.

- **[Lens K8S IDE](https://docs.k8slens.dev/k8slens/)**


* * *


Install Lens K8S IDE, sign up for Lens ID, and start working with your Kubernetes clusters.

- **[Lens Agents](https://docs.k8slens.dev/lens-agents/)**


* * *


The governed platform for running AI agents on enterprise systems — any agent, any model, any environment.

- **[Lens Prism](https://docs.k8slens.dev/k8slens/lens-prism/)**


* * *


Explore Kubernetes clusters with _Lens Prism_, the built-in AI assistant for Lens K8S IDE

- **[Administration](https://docs.k8slens.dev/k8slens/admin/)**


* * *


Manage personal Lens account and administer a Lens Business ID. Configure SSO/SCIM, and manage availability of features for Lens Business ID users.
